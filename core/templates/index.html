Strategy 1: Trend Following

Master the art of trend following to ride the momentum in the forex market. This strategy involves identifying established trends and making trades in the direction of those trends. Key components of trend following include:

  • Identifying trend direction using moving averages
  • Using support and resistance levels for entry and exit points
  • Implementing risk management to protect profits
